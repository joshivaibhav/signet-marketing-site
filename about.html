About Us

We at SIGNET are environment friendly and promote products that are safer to mother earth. Our goal is to reduce contamination of land and water by replacing plastic and thermocol disposables.

What is Bagasse?

Bagasse is the fibrous matter that remains after sugarcane stalks are crushed to extract juice. This residue is converted into pulp and used to manufacture biodegradable disposable products.

Our Manufacturing Process

Using food-grade pulp and precision machines, our products are manufactured in a clean and controlled environment, ensuring safety, strength, and sustainability.
